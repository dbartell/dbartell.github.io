Moto Bets

moto-bets.herokuapp.com

github.com/dbartell/Personal-Project

I wanted to make a fantasy motocross site where fans can log in and pick who they think will win a particular race during the supercross and motocross seasons. You can see all the races you predicted on one page and see all the other users who predicted and their predictions for each contest on a single page. I enjoyed this project because It is something I am passionate about and it was fun. Some good lessons I learned were about findin deeply nested mongo queries and displaying them on the page. It gave me a greater appreciation for mongooseJS and its object modeling.

COR Industries

cor.herokuapp.com

github.com/dbartell/COR-Industries

COR Industries - Website for a local company in Lehi, UT. COR Industries order permits for truck drivers to haul oversized loads across highways state to state. I built a web page for them so their customers can order permits online via this website to speed up the process of getting their permits and their oversized loads to their destination faster. My website cut ordering time by 50%. Mobile Compatible for the drivers to walk around the load and record their dimensions.
